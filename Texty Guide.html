Texty - User Guide

By Jack Freund

About Texty

Texty is a all-in-one text editor. It can be a Python, AppleScript, Java, Ruby, C, C++, and Markdown IDE. You can also make, edit, and save plain text and rich text. It is also easy to use.
You can download it here.

Updating Texty

To update Texty, just click on the  ‘Update’ button. If there is an error,  click here.
